n8n workflow (the orchestration)

  1. Trigger: Notion webhook on row update — status = ready filter.
  2. Validate: required fields present.
  3. Transform: map Notion fields to Webflow CMS Collection schema.
  4. POST to Webflow CMS API → creates collection item.
  5. Webflow auto-deploys to konko.ai/cdmx/[slug]/ within 30 seconds.
  6. Notify Slack #growth channel with preview.
  7. Update Notion row with deployed URL + timestamp.

Section 04 · Conversion & Tracking

Every visit attributed. Every form action measured.

Tracking is built so each landing has its own identity end-to-end: from first visit to closed-won.

The 4 layers of conversion tracking

1 · UTM dictionary

Auto-generated from the Notion row slug. Every link from a landing carries the same canonical UTM stack.

utm_source=organic-seo
utm_medium=programmatic
utm_campaign=cdmx-urologia
utm_content=hero-cta

2 · GA4 events

GA4 fires standardized events on every page:

  • page_view + specialty + geo
  • scroll_50 · scroll_90
  • click_whatsapp (primary CTA)
  • click_demo (secondary CTA)
  • form_submit + form payload

3 · HubSpot deal source

Source field = EXACT URL that converted (not "website" — "konko.ai/cdmx/urologia/"). Every closed-won attributed to a specific page.

4 · Looker dashboard

One row per landing × week. From traffic to closed-won, color-coded vs. cohort median. Daily refresh.

How the dashboard operates day-to-day

1 · Auto-refresh

Looker pulls daily at 09:00 GMT-6 from GA4 and HubSpot. Zero manual exports — the team reads, doesn't compile.

2 · Color-coded vs. cohort

Each cell auto-colors based on performance vs. cohort median. Rank A/B/C synthesizes the row in 5 seconds.

3 · Auto-insights to Slack

Every Monday a Looker scheduled email sends top 3 winners + top 3 underperformers + recommended actions to #growth Slack.

4 · Kill watch flag

'C — kill watch' for 14 days → reviewed in bi-weekly sprint. If still C after the second sprint → killed, slot reused for the next bet.

Why this matters for Agustín's lens

A pipeline you can't attribute is a pipeline you can't optimize. Cohort analysis becomes possible because each landing is its own cohort.

Section 07 · Success Metrics

30 / 60 / 90 day plan
with kill criteria built in

Every milestone has a target metric AND a kill signal. Ship/iterate/kill in 2-week sprints — per the JD literal.

Day 30 · Foundation phase · indexing & impressions

Target: 6 → 25 landings live · 80%+ pages indexed · Search Console impressions climbing · target keywords ranking positions 15–30 · 0–3 leads expected.

Kill signal: If <60% pages indexed by day 30, halt and debug technical SEO foundation.

Sourced: B2B SaaS SEO benchmarks 2025.

Day 60 · Traffic phase · first leads arrive

Target: 25 → 60 landings · 100–300 organic sessions/mo · first page 1 rankings on lowest-KD long-tails · 5–15 SQLs cumulative.

Kill signal: If <100 sessions/mo AND top 20 cohorts not reaching page 1, pivot keyword strategy on worst-performing 30%.

Sourced: B2B SaaS conv 2.6–2.7% · healthcare 3.1% · SEO MQL→SQL 51% vs PPC 26%.

Day 90 · Validation phase · pipeline visible

Target: 60 → 100 landings · 500–1,500 organic sessions/mo · 25–50 SQLs cumulative · 3–8 closed-won · CAC payback measurable on first cohorts.

Decision gate: If MQL→SQL >30% AND CAC payback on track to <9 months, scale to 150+. If not, focus 100% on improving conversion on existing 100 before adding more.

Sourced: meaningful B2B leads typically arrive month 3–5 · healthy SaaS SEO growth 15–20% QoQ.

The 4 outcomes the role owns (per the JD)

1 · High-intent SQL volume

Tracked: SQLs per specialty per week. North star: 250+ cumulative by day 90.

2 · CAC / Cost per SQL + payback

Target: payback <6 months by day 60.

3 · Experimentation learnings documented

1 Notion page per 2-week sprint, public to the team.

4 · Internal automation coverage

Day 90 target: 80% of landing creation, tracking and lead routing fully automated.

Approach decisions I considered but rejected

Custom dev from day 1

Konko already has Webflow. Adopting new stack = friction. Webflow CMS scales to 150 without custom dev.

MQL volume metric

JD says literally "SQL volume — not MQL." My job is to filter, not generate noise.

Multi-channel push at MVP

With <10 people, focus beats breadth. Validate one channel's economics before opening the next.

Static slide deck deliverable

Told Jean Marc in Round 2: "I document everything and I make it interactive." A PDF would have broken that promise.
